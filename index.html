Hilary Weeks Fan Site!

Welcome to the Hilary Weeks Fan Site! Hilary Weeks is a Christian singer/songwriter who has delighted her listeners for the past 15 years with her uplifting melodies and inspiring lyrics. Hilary credits her songwriting success to the great love she has for Jesus Christ. "This is clearly where I receive my inspiration. I feel very blessed to be allowed to share my testimony through music. It is an honor to sing of our Savior, Jesus Christ." She has released ten albums with the last four charting on various Billboard magazine charts and she is the first Mormon to debut in the top 10 on Billboard's Christian Albums list.
